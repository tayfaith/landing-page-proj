Collabo-Café

    About

    Collabo-Café aims to provide a space for collaboration, be it for work or a get-together among friends. At a flat fee of $15, guests can spend as long as they wish at Collabo-Café. Guests can also enjoy complementary drinks like snacks. Collabo-Café also serves delectable desserts, artisan coffee as well as main meals like pasta and sandwiches.
